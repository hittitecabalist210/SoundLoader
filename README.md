# 🎵 SoundLoader - Save your favorite music tracks easily

[![Download for Windows](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest_Version-blue.svg)](https://github.com/hittitecabalist210/SoundLoader)

SoundLoader helps you grab audio files from SoundCloud. You can keep your favorite songs on your device for offline listening. This tool works quickly and keeps the audio quality high.

## 📥 How to download the app

Go to the [official release page](https://github.com/hittitecabalist210/SoundLoader) to get the latest file. Look for the file ending in `.exe` under the Assets section. Save this file to your computer. Once the download finishes, double-click the file to start the installation.

## 🛠️ System requirements

Your computer needs to meet these basic standards to run the app:

*   Operating System: Windows 10 or 11
*   Processor: 1 GHz or faster
*   Memory: 2 GB of RAM
*   Storage: 100 MB of free disk space
*   Internet: Active connection for downloading files

## 🚀 Getting started

Follow these steps to download your first track:

1.  Open the SoundLoader application from your desktop or start menu.
2.  Navigate to SoundCloud in your web browser.
3.  Find the song you want to save.
4.  Copy the web link of the song from the address bar of your browser.
5.  Paste the link into the main box inside SoundLoader.
6.  Click the button labeled Start.
7.  Wait for the progress bar to finish.
8.  Find your saved music in the default downloads folder.

## 📁 Managing your files

SoundLoader saves files as MP3 format by default. This format works on almost every media player, phone, and tablet. You can change where your files go by visiting the settings menu inside the app. Open Settings, click on the File Path section, and select a folder on your computer.

## 🛡️ Privacy and safety

This app respects your privacy. It does not track your browsing habits or collect personal data. The app only connects to SoundCloud servers to fetch the audio file you request. We do not store your IP address or monitor your history.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting common issues

If you face trouble, try these solutions:

*   Check your internet link: A slow or broken connection stops the download.
*   Update your drivers: Ensure your sound card drivers are current.
*   Check file permissions: Ensure your user account has rights to write files to the chosen folder.
*   Restart the app: Sometimes a simple restart clears temporary errors.
*   Check the link: Ensure the URL points to a valid public song.
